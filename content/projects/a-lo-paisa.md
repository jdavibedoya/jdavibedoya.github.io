### Core Architecture

1. **Speech-to-Text (STT):** Transcribes raw audio into text.
2. **Dialect Adaptation (LLM):** An LLM agent restructures the text to match the Paisa dialect while preserving the original meaning.
3. **Neural Synthesis (TTS):** The transformed text is converted back into speech using neural speech synthesis.
